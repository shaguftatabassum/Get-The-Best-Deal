About

The Deal Finder keeps you informed about the minimum price of the products you wish to buy.

The product may be available on various e-commerce sites. It keeps a check on the products

prices and lets you know the minimum price for each product

How to get started

Just go to the add new product tab,enter the required details.And you are done.The price of that

product will be tracked and minimum of all of the prices for the product will be sent as notification

along with the correspoding link. You can have many products and for each product you can add

many sites.
